Coffee Maker

automatic_brew: (boolean)
bean_grinder: (boolean)
cups_able_to_brew: (integer)
warmer: (boolean)

set_brew_time: (Brews coffee at a user-selected time. Uses automatic_brew attribute.)
grind_beans: (Grinds beans so they are fresh when making coffee. Uses bean_grinder attribute.)
brew_coffee: (Brews coffee. Uses the cups_able_to_brew attribute.)
keep_coffee_warm: (Keeps coffee warm after being brewed. Uses warmer attribute.)
